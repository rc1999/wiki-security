## Security Contribution (May 2026)

Ripple became the first major crypto firm to share its **internal North Korean threat intelligence** externally through Crypto ISAC's API.

**Data shared:**
- Fraud-linked domains
- DPRK-linked wallet addresses
- IOCs from active hack campaigns
- Enriched operative profiles (LinkedIn, email, location, phone, correlated campaign signals)

**Detection method:** Sophisticated AI-enhanced detection workflows

**Why it matters:** Previously, this level of internal threat intelligence was never shared externally among crypto firms. Ripple's move established a precedent for collective defense.
