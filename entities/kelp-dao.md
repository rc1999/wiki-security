# Kelp DAO

**Ethereum re-staking platform. Lost approximately $292 million in a North Korean social engineering infiltration in early 2026.**

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Type** | Re-staking platform / DeFi protocol |
| **Blockchain** | Ethereum |
| **Loss** | ~$292 million |
| **Attribution** | Lazarus Group (DPRK) |
| **When** | Early 2026 |

## The Attack

Similar to the [[drift-protocol]] attack — not a code exploit, but **insider infiltration:**

- North Korean operatives gained access through social engineering
- Compromised internal systems/wallets
- Extracted ~$292 million in assets

## Combined Impact with Drift

Together with Drift ($285M), these two incidents represent:
- **$577 million** in total losses
- **76%** of all crypto hack value in 2026 (through April)
- The primary driver for [[crypto-isac]]'s enhanced threat intelligence sharing initiative
